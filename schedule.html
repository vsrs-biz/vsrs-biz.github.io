Wedding schedule

The wedding goes from 6p-11p on July 16th at the MCA in Chicago. We look forward to seeing you there! The outdoor ceremony starts at 6p, followed by an outdoor cocktail hour at ~620p. Dinner (outdoor) and dancing (indoor) follow the cocktail hour. All outdoor activities with the exception of the ceremony are under a tent; in case of rain, the ceremony will take place indoors, but the cocktail hour and dinner will still take place outdoors.
